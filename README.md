## Quizzo , AI-Powered Exam Builder & Assessment Platform
**Quizzo is not just a quiz app; it's a comprehensive tool for educators to create digital and printable exams or quizzes. Versatile platform designed to meet all testing and evaluation needs, whether digital or on paper. It serves two main purposes:**


* **Online Quiz Platform:** Users can create, share, and solve interactive quizzes on any topic, compete on leaderboards, and track their performance dynamically.
* **Professional Exam Builder:** A powerful tool for educators to generate printable A4 exam sheets. It utilizes an AI Question Wizard to generate content from text/files and an Interactive Canvas (powered by Interact.js) for drag-and-drop layout customization.
